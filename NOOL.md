# NOOL: arangam-space-kit

## Intent (நோக்கம்)

**WHY does this repository exist?**

To provide the open-source companion for the ARANGAM OS cognitive operating system. This kit makes the philosophy, curriculum, and thinking templates freely available so that anyone can build their own cognitive operating system, whether they use ARANGAM OS or not.

The deeper intent is to cultivate **independent thinkers**, not SYNTHAI imitators. Every template is designed to force localization: the user must translate the structure into their own context, language, and reality.

## Abstraction (வடிவம்)

**WHAT TYPE of artifact is this?**

This is a **public OSS teaching toolkit**, structured as three layers:
1. **Methodology** (the WHY): Philosophy documents explaining the principles
2. **Curriculum** (the WHAT): Video transcripts and diagrams from the YouTube series
3. **Kit** (the HOW): Blank thinking templates for immediate use

It follows the KIT pattern established by `spar-kit`: a public companion to a private product.

## Chain (சங்கிலி)

**HOW will we know this succeeded?**

1. Viewers can watch a YouTube video and immediately use the matching template
2. Users can operate the Kit without ever using ARANGAM OS
3. Contributors translate templates into other languages
4. No one copies our exact setup; they build their own version
5. The Kit naturally creates demand for the automated ARANGAM OS experience
