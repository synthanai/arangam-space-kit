> Session 1, Pillar 1: Know Yourself | Build Your Legacy | ~4 min

Quick. Without thinking. Tell me: who are you?

If the first thing that came to mind was your job title, we have a problem.

## The Problem (0:15 - 1:30)

Here's something that happens to almost every ambitious professional I've met.

They can tell you what they DO. "I'm a consultant." "I run a design studio." "I'm in sustainability."

They can tell you what they've DONE. "I've been in the industry for fifteen years." "I gave a talk at that conference."

But they cannot tell you WHY they do what they do.

Not the polished answer. Not the one they rehearsed for investors. The real one.

"Why are YOU the person who does THIS work?"

Silence.

I was the same. For years, I defined myself by output: projects, roles, achievements. Then one day, someone asked me a question I couldn't answer:

*"What is the work you were born to do?"*

Not your current job. Not what pays the bills. What is the pattern you keep returning to, regardless of the context? What do you solve for people even when nobody asks you to?

I didn't have an answer. And I realized: I had never sat down and written it.

Most of us haven't. We inherit our identity from our degrees, our companies, our LinkedIn headlines. We never author it ourselves.

---

## The Principle (1:30 - 2:45)

Your identity isn't a job title. It has four layers.

**Layer 1: Your Calling.** The pattern you keep returning to. The problem you solve even when nobody asks. The thing you were doing the last time you completely lost track of time. Not your job, your gravity.

**Layer 2: Your Origin.** Where did that calling come from? What life experience forged it? Every person I've worked with has a wound or a struggle that became their greatest strength. Your origin is not your resume. It's the story your resume is trying to hide.

**Layer 3: Your Reason.** Why does it matter beyond you? If you disappeared tomorrow, what gap would you leave? Who specifically benefits when you operate at your best? This is the layer that turns ambition into purpose.

**Layer 4: Your Endurance.** What keeps you going when it gets hard? What will you never compromise on? When you wanted to quit in the past, what pulled you back?

Four layers. Calling. Origin. Reason. Endurance.

Most professionals have never written these down. Not because they don't matter, but because nobody ever asked the right questions.

## Your Turn (3:15 - 3:45)

Pause this video.

Open the template linked in the description. It has 12 questions, three for each layer.

Don't copy someone else's answers. Don't write what sounds impressive. Write what's true.

The anti-imitation rule: if your answers sound like they could belong to anyone, they belong to no one. Make them unmistakably yours.

When you're done, write one sentence that captures all four layers. That's your identity anchor.
