# The Steal Kit

**Blank thinking templates for building your cognitive operating system.**

> ⚠️ **STEAL THIS, DON'T COPY IT.**
> These templates are thinking structures, not answer sheets.
> The headings are universal. The content must be YOURS.

---

## How to Use

1. Pick a template that matches what you need right now
2. Copy it to your own system (Obsidian, Notion, Google Docs, or paper)
3. Read the guiding questions carefully
4. Fill it in with YOUR context, YOUR people, YOUR language
5. Review and refine over time

## Categories

| Folder | What It's For | Start Here If... |
|--------|---------------|------------------|
| `/identity` | Understanding who you are and what you stand for | You've never written down your values |
| `/capture` | Catching signals and observations from the world | You miss important information regularly |
| `/thinking` | Structuring your reasoning and examining your biases | You make decisions on gut feeling alone |
| `/decisions` | Making traceable, reversible, auditable decisions | You can't explain why you decided something |
| `/operations` | Building and maintaining organizational systems | Your team depends on heroes, not processes |
| `/reflection` | Learning from what happened and evolving | You keep making the same mistakes |

## The Anti-Imitation Standard

Every template in this kit follows three rules:

1. **Questions, Not Answers**: Templates contain prompts, not examples
2. **Localization Required**: You must translate every concept to your reality
3. **No Jargon**: If a SYNTHAI term appears, a plain-English alternative is provided
