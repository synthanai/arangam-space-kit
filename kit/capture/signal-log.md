# Signal Log

> ⚠️ **STEAL THIS, DON'T COPY IT.**
> This is YOUR observation journal, not a copy of ours.
> The three columns (Source, Signal, So-What) are universal.
> What you notice is uniquely yours.

## Anti-Imitation Check
1. Am I capturing signals from MY world, or just bookmarking interesting articles?
2. Does each "So-What" connect to something I'm actually working on?
3. Am I observing, or just consuming?

---

## How to Use

Every day (or at least weekly), capture what you noticed. Not what you read, but what made you stop and think. The goal is to train your perception.
