# Decision Record

> ⚠️ **STEAL THIS, DON'T COPY IT.**
> This is a thinking tool for decisions that matter.
> If the decision is trivial, don't use this template. Save it for the ones you'll have to explain later.

## Anti-Imitation Check
1. Is this a real decision I'm facing, not a hypothetical exercise?
2. Have I listed options I'm genuinely considering, not just a straw man?
3. Am I writing this BEFORE the decision, not after (to justify it)?
