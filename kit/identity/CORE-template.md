# CORE Profile Template

> ⚠️ **STEAL THIS, DON'T COPY IT.**
> This is a self-discovery structure. There are no right answers.
> Do not copy someone else's CORE. This must come from your own reflection.

## Anti-Imitation Check
Before filling this in, ask yourself:
1. Am I being honest, or am I writing what sounds impressive?
2. Would someone who knows me well recognize this as authentically ME?
3. Am I using my own words, or borrowing aspirational language from others?
