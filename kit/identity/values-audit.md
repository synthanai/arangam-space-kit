# Values Audit

> ⚠️ **STEAL THIS, DON'T COPY IT.**
> Your values are not aspirational posters on the wall.
> They are the things you actually do when nobody is watching.

## Anti-Imitation Check
1. Are these values I actually live by, or values I wish I had?
2. Can I point to a decision I made in the last month that proves each value?
3. Would my team/family agree these are real, not performative?
