# Change Plan

> ⚠️ **STEAL THIS, DON'T COPY IT.**
> Most change fails because leaders focus on the WHAT and ignore the WHO.
> This template forces you to think about the human side of change.

## Anti-Imitation Check
1. Am I planning a real change, not a theoretical one?
2. Have I honestly named who will resist this and why?
3. Am I addressing identity threats, not just logistics?
