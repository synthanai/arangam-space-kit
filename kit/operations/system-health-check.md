# System Health Check

> ⚠️ **STEAL THIS, DON'T COPY IT.**
> This template diagnoses where your team or organization sits on the
> 5 Stages of Systemic Competence. Be brutally honest.

## Anti-Imitation Check
1. Am I assessing MY actual team/org, not an imaginary ideal?
2. Am I being honest about which stage we're really at?
3. Would my team agree with my assessment, or would they rate us differently?

---

## The 5 Stages of Systemic Competence

| Stage | Name | Symptom | Your Team? |
|-------|------|---------|------------|
| 1 | **Unconscious Incompetence** | "We don't have a problem." Heroes save the day. No one sees the system is missing. | ☐ |
| 2 | **Conscious Incompetence** | "We bought Jira/Notion but nothing changed." You know the system is broken, but tools alone aren't fixing it. | ☐ |
| 3 | **Conscious Competence** | "Our processes work, but they're exhausting." Heavy meetings, rigid SOPs, everything requires manual oversight. | ☐ |
| 4 | **Unconscious Competence** | "Things just work." High trust, implicit coordination, the system runs itself. | ☐ |
| 5 | **Competent Incompetence** | "We're the best, nothing left to learn." Success has made the team resistant to new ideas. The Innovator's Trap. | ☐ |

**Check the stage that best describes your team RIGHT NOW.**
