# Dialectic Template

> ⚠️ **STEAL THIS, DON'T COPY IT.**
> Do NOT use the default persona names below. Replace them with
> the voices that actually argue in YOUR head or YOUR boardroom.

## Anti-Imitation Check
1. Have I defined voices that represent real tensions in MY context?
2. Am I genuinely exploring disagreement, or just validating what I already believe?
3. Would each voice be uncomfortable with the other voices' positions?
