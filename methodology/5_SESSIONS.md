# The 5 Sessions

**Five depth levels that take you from awareness to mastery.**

---

## Overview

The curriculum is taught across 5 sessions. Each session covers all 7 pillars, but at an increasing depth. You don't need to master Session 1 before starting Session 2, but Session 1 gives you the vocabulary and tools to go deeper.

---

## Session 1: The Mirror 🪞

**Metaphor**: Seeing yourself clearly for the first time.

**Depth**: Beginner. You are moving from "I don't know what I don't know" to "I can see the problem."

**What You Get**: A starter toolkit. One template per pillar. Enough to begin diagnosing your current state.

**The Story**: An organization that couldn't see itself. Seven specific failures, one per pillar.

---

## Session 2: The Lens 🔍

**Metaphor**: Focusing your perception with active tools.

**Depth**: Practitioner. You can now use specific instruments to examine problems.

**What You Get**: Structured protocols. You stop relying on intuition and start using frameworks with intention.

**The Story**: An organization that could see the problem but kept reaching for the wrong tools.

---

## Session 3: The Map 🗺️

**Metaphor**: Navigating complexity with a shared framework.

**Depth**: Applied. You can map your reality to the frameworks and teach others to do the same.

**What You Get**: Integration skills. You connect the pillars to each other and begin seeing the system, not just the parts.

**The Story**: An organization that had all the right tools but couldn't make them work together.

---

## Session 4: The Blueprint 📐

**Metaphor**: Designing the system from scratch.

**Depth**: Architect. You can design organizational infrastructure using the principles.

**What You Get**: Design capability. You stop fixing problems and start building systems that prevent them.

**The Story**: An organization that redesigned itself from the ground up and what it cost.

---

## Session 5: The Flame 🔥

**Metaphor**: Mastery and the danger of hubris.

**Depth**: Master. You know the system so well that your greatest threat is complacency.

**What You Get**: The meta-view. You learn to audit your own mastery, detect blind spots at the highest level, and teach others.

**The Story**: An organization that mastered the system and was destroyed by its own success.

---

## The Progression

| Session | Maturity | You Go From... | To... |
|---------|----------|----------------|-------|
| Mirror | S → P | Not seeing the problem | Recognizing the problem |
| Lens | P → A | Recognizing the problem | Using the right tools |
| Map | A | Using tools | Mapping the whole system |
| Blueprint | A → R | Mapping the system | Designing the architecture |
| Flame | R → K | Designing architecture | Mastering and transcending |
